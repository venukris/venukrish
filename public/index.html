Crafting this space bit by bit..

Hello! 👋 I’m Venu, a Senior Software Engineer based in Wellington, New Zealand. I specialize in developing tools and pipeline that empower artists to create digital visual effects for film and television. At Weta FX, my focus is with the Creatures Department, where I help bring realistic creatures in epic fantasies like Avatar: The Way of Water and Kingdom of the Planet of the Apes to life. You can read about my career and journey into the VFX industry here.

I envision this website as a hybrid space, blending my professional work and personal projects with my interests in investing, travel, books, and podcasts, alongside various musings on life.
